Privacy Policy for SwapUnits Browser Extension

Last Updated: December 4, 2025

Overview

SwapUnits is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension is designed to work entirely on your device with minimal data collection.

Data Collection

What We Collect

  • Conversion Preferences: Your preferred target units for each category (e.g., USDâ†’EUR) are stored locally using chrome.storage.sync
  • Dark Mode Preference: Your theme choice is stored locally
  • No Personal Information: We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal information, browsing history, or identifiable data

How Data is Stored

  • All preferences are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API
  • Data syncs across your Chrome devices if you're signed into Chrome (via Chrome's built-in sync)
  • You can clear this data at any time by removing the extension

Third-Party Services

Currency Exchange Rates

  • Currency conversions use the free API from exchangerate-api.com
  • Only currency codes and amounts are sent (e.g., "USD to EUR, value: 100")
  • No personal information is transmitted
  • This service has its own privacy policy: https://www.exchangerate-api.com/terms

Other Conversions

  • All other unit conversions (length, mass, temperature, etc.) are calculated locally on your device
  • No data is sent to any server

Permissions Explained

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • activeTab: Allows the extension to read selected text on the current page to detect units
  • storage: Saves your conversion preferences locally
  • contextMenus: (Future feature) Will add right-click menu options
  • host_permissions (exchangerate-api.com): Required only for live currency conversion rates

Data Usage

We use your data solely to:

  1. Remember your preferred conversion units
  2. Provide accurate currency exchange rates
  3. Maintain your dark mode preference

We do NOT:

  • Sell or share your data
  • Track your browsing
  • Collect analytics
  • Show ads
  • Require account creation

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all stored data by removing the extension
  • Clear preferences via the extension settings
  • Use the extension without providing any personal information

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as needed. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
